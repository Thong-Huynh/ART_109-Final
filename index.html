Past-Present-Future

Artist Statement

For this project, I create a site to showcase a breakdown of a motion design project I completed in other class this semester. The project is inspired by the Japanese elements (bonsai, fuurin - wind chime, koi fish,...) and Zen philosophy to visualize a warm summer breeze. The vintage film style to convey a nostalgic and historical sense of the time that has been long gone. In the website, I set up a space with light background wrapped around by a geometric net-texture to give impression of a futuristic look that suggests a space or a domain inside one's mind. Player, who represents the present, will be able to move around a space with one straight path that contains layers of images, and walk through them in the same way that one might look through layers of cut out papers or just as the experience of riding a amusement ride.
